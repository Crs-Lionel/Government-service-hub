Common Questions

1. How do I file my income tax online?
You can file your income tax online through the official Income Tax India e-filing portal.

You will need your PAN card, Form 16, and other income-related documents.
3. Is it safe to file taxes online?
Yes, the Income Tax India e-filing portal is secure and encrypted.
4. How long does it take to process my tax return?
Tax returns are generally processed within a few weeks, depending on your case.
5. Can I track the status of my tax refund?
Yes, you can track your tax refund status through the e-filing portal.
